Welcome to Honey Dew

We’re passionate about bringing the authentic taste of Kerala’s beloved Nannari Sarbath right to your glass.

Our Story

Founded in [Year], Honey Dew was born out of a love for Kerala’s traditional flavors and a commitment to quality. Our founder, Baiju Raghav, envisioned a way to share the refreshing and healthful properties of Nannari Sarbath with the world. What began as a small local endeavor has grown into a beloved brand, dedicated to preserving and celebrating this timeless drink.

Our Process

We believe in quality and authenticity. Each bottle of our Nannari Sarbath syrup is made using traditional methods combined with modern quality controls. From selecting the best nannari roots to ensuring every batch meets our rigorous standards, we ensure that our syrup delivers the true essence of Kerala’s heritage.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic Flavor: Our syrup captures the true taste of Kerala, offering a refreshing and cooling experience.
  • Natural Ingredients: We use only natural nannari roots and pure ingredients in our syrup.
  • Health Benefits: Nannari Sarbath is known for its cooling properties and potential health benefits, making it a great choice for a refreshing and wholesome drink.

Ingredients

Nannari Roots

The primary ingredient in our syrup, known for its cooling properties and unique flavor.

Natural Sugar

We use pure, natural sugar to balance the flavor and enhance the taste of our syrup.

Purified Water

Essential for the preparation of our syrup, ensuring purity and a smooth texture.
